Long Haul was my team's entry into the 2021 48 hour film challenge here. I acted as Directory of Photography. While my primary responsibilities were related to the camera work, I also helped write and storyboard the project.

The short film is science fiction, covering the story of a long-haul delivery driver as they struggle with the grim reality of being stranded out in the middle of space. By chance, he uses some of the cargo on his ship to meet a new friend.
